About

Welcome! My name is Ryan Kowalewski and this is my blog.

I am a Virtualization/Cloud Technical Specialist in the Platforms team of a FTSE 100 company based on the south coast of the UK. My role mainly focusses on VMware, Azure and automation, and as such many of my posts will be in these areas, although I will throw in some different content from time to time.

I regularly find myself referring to many of the awesome blogs/resources which already exist in the community and as such this is my way of giving a little bit back while hopefully helping a few people along the way.

Thanks for reading!
